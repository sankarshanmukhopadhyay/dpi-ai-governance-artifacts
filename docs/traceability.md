# TRACE Control → Artifact Traceability

This document maps TRACE controls (from the Lab) to concrete artifacts in this repository.

> This is intentionally lightweight in v0.1.0. Expand coverage incrementally.

## Trust

- **TRACE-TRUST-01** — Decision rights and accountability are explicitly defined.  
  Artifacts: `templates/governance/decision-rights.md`, `templates/governance/raci.md`

- **TRACE-TRUST-03** — Redress exists (appeal paths, timelines, enforcement).  
  Artifacts: `templates/redress/redress-workflow.md`, `schemas/redress/redress-workflow.schema.json`

## Risk

- **TRACE-RISK-01** — Risk register exists with ownership, likelihood/impact, and treatment.  
  Artifacts: `templates/risk/risk-register.md`, `schemas/risk/risk-register.schema.json`

## Architecture

- **TRACE-ARC-02** — Critical dependencies are enumerated (compute/model/vendor/legal).  
  Artifacts: `templates/architecture/dependency-inventory.md`

## Conformance

- **TRACE-CONF-01** — Claims are backed by artifacts and evidence expectations.  
  Artifacts: `conformance/evidence-bundle.md`
