# Schedule of controlled documents

This index is the authoritative navigation list of controlled documents in this repository.

## Controlled document categories

### Risk
- `controlled/risk/risk-register.md` — risk register and treatment planning (informative + normative fields).
- `controlled/risk/risk-scoring-matrix.md` — scoring approach and calibration guidance (informative).

### Assurance
- `controlled/assurance/evidence-bundles.md` — evidence bundle expectations per assurance level / tier (normative).
- `controlled/assurance/tier-profiles/` — tier-specific profiles that bind requirements to contexts (normative where stated).
- `controlled/assurance/transparency-scorecard.md` — transparency scoring guidance (informative; may include normative elements if labeled).

### Redress
- `controlled/redress/redress-and-remediation.md` — escalation, appeal, remediation completion evidence (normative).

### Technical / Information trust / Inclusion / Legal
Reserved for expansion:
- `controlled/technical/`
- `controlled/info-trust/`
- `controlled/inclusion-accessibility/`
- `controlled/legal/`

## Normativity conventions

Each controlled document SHOULD declare whether it is normative, informative, or mixed.
For mixed documents, normative statements MUST be explicitly marked and use RFC 2119 keywords.
