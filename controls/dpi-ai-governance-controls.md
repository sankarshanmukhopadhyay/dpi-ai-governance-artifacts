# DPI-AI Governance Control Catalog

This catalog provides a compact set of canonical controls intended to bridge policy language and operational assurance.

## Included controls

- `DPI-AI-CTRL-001` — Mandate and legal authority definition
- `DPI-AI-CTRL-002` — Risk register maintenance
- `DPI-AI-CTRL-003` — Evidence bundle completeness
- `DPI-AI-CTRL-004` — Decision notice and redress
- `DPI-AI-CTRL-005` — Population-scale monitoring

## Why this matters

Frameworks multiply. Deployable controls remain scarce. The purpose of this catalog is to give implementers a stable surface that can be referenced by schemas, evidence bundles, profiles, and review workflows without re-litigating the entire philosophy of governance every Tuesday.
