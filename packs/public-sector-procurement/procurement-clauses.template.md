These are **starter clauses** to anchor governance expectations into a contract.
They are not jurisdiction-specific legal advice.

## 1) Artifact production and maintenance

Supplier SHALL maintain and provide the following artifacts throughout the contract term:
- Decision receipt specification and sample outputs
- Authority and delegation records
- Rulebook manifest and change-control records
- Redress workflow documentation and SLA metrics
- Incident reporting and audit finding records (as applicable)

## 2) Change control

Supplier SHALL notify the Buyer of material changes to governance-relevant rules, models, or workflows, and SHALL
provide a change record aligned to `templates/rulebook-change-control.template.md` (or equivalent).

## 3) Redress and remediation

Supplier SHALL provide an appeal and redress mechanism for impacted individuals and SHALL measure correction SLAs
using a documented method.

## 4) Validation and audit rights

Supplier SHALL provide schema-validatable artifacts where schemas exist in this repository and SHALL support
audit requests for verification of claims.

## 5) Supply chain transparency

Supplier SHALL provide a vendor capability attestation and SHALL disclose relevant dependencies and supply-chain
controls, including material sub-processors that impact the DPI+AI system.
